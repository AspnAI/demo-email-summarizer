Jazz - Dunya Ko Bataa Do! is a telecommunications company specializing in providing a wide range of mobile services. They offer prepaid and postpaid plans, 4G devices, and a variety of mobile apps. Their services include data plans with varying GB limits, voice over WiFi services, and a smartphone installment plan. They also offer a self-service option through their Jazz World app and WhatsApp. Unique to their services is the "Bookme Bro" service for Jazz customers and the Jazz x Kistpay smartphone installment plan. Their impact in the industry is significant, offering comprehensive mobile solutions and innovative services like the first Voice over WiFi service in Pakistan.
